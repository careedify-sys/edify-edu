## Why Competitors Win (Pattern Analysis)

### 1. Content Depth
- Top rankers average 2000-3000 words. Several EdifyEdu blogs are 4-8 min reads (~800-1500 words).
- University review blogs need 2000+ words with fee tables, syllabus sections, placement data, and FAQs.

### 2. Structured Data
- CollegeVidya and samarthedu use Review schema, FAQ schema, and PriceSpecification.
- EdifyEdu uses Article + FAQ schema but lacks Review and PriceSpecification.

### 3. Multiple SERP Slots
- CollegeVidya creates both hub pages AND blog reviews for the same university, capturing 2-3 positions.
- EdifyEdu has /universities/{slug} pages but they don't rank for review queries.

### 4. Freshness Signals
- Top rankers update content monthly with "Updated [Month] 2026" tags.
- Several EdifyEdu blogs are dated March 2026 with no update signal.

### 5. Backlink Profile
- Shiksha/Careers360 have massive domain authority from their parent media companies.
- Smaller affiliates (collegevidya, samarthedu) build links through university partnerships.
- EdifyEdu's independent positioning limits partnership-driven backlinks but enables trust-based differentiation.

## EdifyEdu's Unleveraged Advantages

1. **128-university database with real NIRF/NAAC data** — no competitor has this depth
2. **Interactive tools (EMI calculator, compare tool)** — not integrated into blog content
3. **Independent positioning (no paid rankings)** — differentiator vs affiliate sites
4. **Specialization-level data (304 spec pages)** — unique depth no competitor matches
5. **Fee comparison across 128 universities** — data exists but isn't surfaced for fee queries
