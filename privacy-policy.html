This Privacy Policy describes how Rishi Upadhyay ("I", "me", or "my"), operating as an independent Education Counsellor, collects, uses, and protects the information you provide when you use this website.

When you fill the enquiry form on this website, I collect the following information:

The information you provide is used solely for the following purposes:
