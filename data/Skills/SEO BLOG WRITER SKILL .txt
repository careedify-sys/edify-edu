# SEO Blog Writer Skill

## Purpose
Generate SEO-optimized, human-sounding long-form content for EdifyEdu and similar
editorial sites. Focus on ranking in Indian education SERP while sounding like a
real editor, not a content farm.

## Output structure for every blog

Every generated post includes:

1. **SEO Title** (50-60 chars, includes focus keyword, year 2026 when relevant)
2. **Meta Description** (150-160 chars, compelling CTA, focus keyword in first 100 chars)
3. **Focus Keyword** (primary term we target)
4. **LSI Keywords** (3-5 related terms to sprinkle naturally)
5. **Tags** (5-8 categorical tags)
6. **Internal CTAs** (2-3 links to relevant EdifyEdu pages)
7. **Body content** (prose sections, no excessive headers)

## Writing rules

### Tone
- Confident editorial voice, not promotional
- Write like a human with opinions who happens to know the subject
- Reader is a working Indian adult considering an online degree
- Assume intelligence; do not over-explain basic concepts

### Forbidden patterns
- Em dashes (—) use hyphens or restructure
- "In this article we will explore"
- "Let's dive into"
- "Here are some key points"
- "It's important to note that"
- "Stay tuned"
- "Without further ado"
- Rule of three listicles when prose works better
- Perfect parallel structure in every paragraph

### Structure
- Open with a specific, concrete observation, not a generic definition
- Second paragraph addresses the reader's actual question
- Use subheads sparingly — only where they earn their place
- Vary sentence length: mix 6-word punches with 22-word explanations
- Paragraph length: 2-5 sentences, never walls of text

### SEO hygiene
- Focus keyword in H1, first paragraph, and one H2
- LSI keywords appear naturally in body
- No keyword stuffing
- Internal links use descriptive anchor text, not "click here"
- Images must have alt text describing the image, not the keyword

### Honesty markers (EdifyEdu specific)
- Fees always marked "indicative, confirm with counsellor"
- No fabricated statistics
- Sources cited when making claims (deb.ugc.ac.in, nirfindia.org, university official sites)
- Red flags stated honestly, not buried
- EdifyEdu's commission-free position mentioned once, not repeatedly

## Research step

Before writing, do a quick SERP scan for the focus keyword. Look at:
- Top 3 ranking pages (what they cover, what they miss)
- People Also Ask box (common follow-up questions)
- Related searches (intent variants)

Use this research to identify a gap or angle competitors miss. Write to fill that gap.

## Length targets

- Short blog: 800-1200 words
- Standard blog: 1500-2000 words
- Pillar content: 2500-3500 words
- University review: 2500-3000 words

## Apply humanizer skill after drafting

Every blog generated with this skill must be run through the humanizer skill before
finalizing. The humanizer catches AI tell-tale patterns and replaces them.
